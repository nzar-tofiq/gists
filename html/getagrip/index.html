Introduction

The game of table tennis was first played in the 1880s and has become bigger and bigger ever since. The game of table tennis, or ping-pong, is a sport in which two or four players hit a lightweight ball over a net trying to win points. Below is the introductory video to table tennis. Please watch and enjoy.

The Basics

Find someone to play with. You probably want to begin by playing someone who is at about your skill level or a little better, and preferably someone who isn't aggressively competitive. Failing that you can build a cutting edge machine to play against(as depicted below). This way, you can have fun learning how to play. You can play one-on-one, or you can play with two teams of two, which is known as doubles. And you want someone who has regulation ping pong balls, paddles, and a table if you don't have access to any!
